How to make a Low-Cal Chocolate-Peanut Butter Smoothie

Ingredients

  • Quick Weight Loss Creamy Chocolate Powder
  • 2/3 cup Jif Peanut Butter
  • 1/4 cup Lactose Free Milk
  • 8oz Water
  • 1 cup Ice

Click Images for Instructions

Step 1: Gather all Ingredients
Step 2: Pour 1 cup of ice into blender
Step 3: Put 2/3 cup of Peanut butter into blender
Step 4: Pour Creamy Chocolate powder into blender
Step 5: Pour 1/4 cup of Lactose free milk into blender
Step 6: Pour 8 oz of water into blender
Step 7: Blend ingredients on High for 30-45 seconds
Step 8: Pour your smoothie into a cup & enjoy!
